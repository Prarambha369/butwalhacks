Frequently Asked Questions

How can I join HackClub Butwal?

Simply fill out our contact form above or join our Discord/Slack community. We welcome all skill levels!

Do I need programming experience?

Not at all! We have workshops for complete beginners as well as advanced developers. Everyone is welcome.

Are your events free?

Yes! All our workshops, meetups, and events are completely free for participants.

How often do you organize events?

We typically host 2-3 events per month, including workshops, hackathons, and community meetups.
